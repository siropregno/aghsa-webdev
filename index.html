Opti-Etch

Product Overview

Opti-Etch is Luxfer Graphic Arts' new etching oil for use with magnesium photo-engraving plates. Opti-Etch holds fine detail whilst maintaining optimal etch rates and prolongs bath life.

Operating Guidance

Opti-Etch has been developed to directly replace the existing etching oils and can be used with Luxfer Graphic Arts' recommended standard parameters.
